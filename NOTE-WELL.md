## 1. Public Nature of Contributions

All contributions including, but not limited to, reviews, comments, issues, pull requests, are:

- public
- permanent
- archived in Git history
- licensed under the MIT License

Do **not** submit sensitive, private, or proprietary information.

---

## 2. Licensing Obligations

By participating, you agree that:

- Your contributions are licensed under the MIT License.
- You grant maintainers the right to edit, modify, or remove your contributions.
- You have the legal right to submit any material you provide.

---

## 3. Behavioral Expectations

All participants must follow the **Code of Conduct**.
Expectations of respectful and constructive behavior reflect the standards described in RFC 7154.

---

## 4. Moderation and Enforcement

All activity may be moderated according to **MODERATION.md**.
Moderators and maintainers may take action to preserve community integrity.

---

## 5. Consensus and Decision-Making

This Project uses a form of **rough consensus**, inspired by the description in RFC 7282:

- Agreement is determined by reasoned feedback, not voting.
- Strong objections must be substantive and evidence-based.
- Silence does not imply approval.
